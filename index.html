How to use your signature

  • Gmail: Settings → See all settings → General → Signature → Paste
  • Outlook: Settings → Mail → Compose and reply → Email signature → Paste
  • Apple Mail: Preferences → Signatures → Paste (uncheck "Always match font")

Use "Copy as Rich Text" for Gmail/Outlook, or "Copy HTML" for clients that accept raw HTML.
